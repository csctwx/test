Call & Content Settings
Caller ID Blocking

NOTE: If caller ID Blocking is enabled,all calls will be affected.To block your number for the next call only,simply dial *67 followed by the 10-digit number you're calling.At the end of that call, your caller ID will resume display.

International Calling

This settings allows you to make international calls.Note that international calling may be charged at a different rate.To see a full list of rates visit the main site.

Content Filter

When enabled by the account holder, the Content Filter will restrict access to harmful, obscene or otherwise inappropriate internet content from their device.
